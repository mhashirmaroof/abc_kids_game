Privacy Policy

ABC Kids: Tap & Learn

Last updated: May 9, 2026

ABC Kids: Tap & Learn ("the App") is developed by Hashir Maroof ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details.

All progress (star count, selected mode) is stored locally on your device only using Flutter's SharedPreferences and is never transmitted anywhere.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

ABC Kids: Tap & Learn is designed for children ages 3–6 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • No name, email, photo, location, or any other personal data is collected.
  • No user accounts or profiles are created.
  • No social features, chat, or third-party sharing of child data.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. For children's content, AdMob is configured to serve non-personalised ads only (COPPA-compliant mode).

To learn more about how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — required only to load AdMob advertisements. The app works fully offline without ads.

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage.

5. Data Storage

All data (star count, app state) is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — advertising (AdMob Privacy)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
